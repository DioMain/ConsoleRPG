Once in the Gym

A long time ago, four nations lived in peace, but the gym people started a war.
Only the "Player", the lord of three hundred bucks could end this.

Player

This site is a preview of our project on the Basics of Software Engineering.
Our game is made in C++, for development we have assembled a team of 4 people and distributed responsibilities.
This is how we learned to work with GitHub and work in a team.

Our adventure game is made in the console, has the function of changing colors, saving \ loading, battles, and events.
The project is still under development, but we are trying.
